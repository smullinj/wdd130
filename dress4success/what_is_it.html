What is Family Tied?

Family Tied is all about joining families together that you may not know you even had. Have you ever been stuck in your geneology? Family Tied is a site that will help you get unstuck as well as help you fill in those gaps you can't fine. It is as simple as entering your family history and it will send you the info to people who have the same history. You will never get stuck again.
